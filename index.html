What is a browser?

A browser allows you to retrieve videos, texts and images from all over the world through the internet. You can't reach a website without a web browser. To use a browser you will need a web address to admit to the browser and when you do that the browser displays the website for you on your computure or phone. The most used web browsers are Google Chrome, Apple Safari, Firefox and Internet Explorer.
